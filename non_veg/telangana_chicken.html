Heat a dry frying pan on the low heat. Add the dried chillies, coriander seeds, cumin seeds and peppercorns. Roast on a low flame for 8-10 minutes make sure the shake the pan often. Now add the poppy seeds and stir well for 2-3 minutes. Take the pan off the heat and cool. Grind to a fine powder in a spice grinder or mixer and set aside. In the same pan add the grated coconut and dry fry for 10 minutes. Stir well. Cool add to grind to a smooth paste with 50mls water. Set aside. Grind the ginger and garlic to a smooth paste and set aside.
